Pancakes

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour: 1/2 of glass
Eggs: 3
Sugar: 3 spoons
Salt: a pinch
Butter: 100 g

Step 1: Sift the dry ingredients together.
Step 2: Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
Step 3: Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip. Continue cooking until brown on both sides.

Pasta

  • Time: 2h
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour: 2 glasses
Eggs: 3
Olive oil: 2 spoons
Salt: a spoon

Step 1: Make a nest with the flour on a clean work surface. Use your hands to gently mix in the flour.
Step 2: When the dough comes together, shape it into a ball and wrap it in plastic wrap.
Step 3: Finally, cut and cook the pasta.

Frappuccino

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Espresso: 50 ml
Milk: 1/2 of glass
Sweetener: a spoon
Ice: 4 cubes
Whipped cream: some

Step 1: Brew the espresso or coffee.
Step 2: In a blender, combine the espresso or coffee, ice, milk, and sweetener(s) of choice. See the recipe card below for exact measurements. Blend well until almost foamy.
Step 3: Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream.
